Here’s the **full description of the Wix Products CSV export format** so your developer can build endpoints for ingestion:

***

## ✅ **File Format**

*   **Type:** CSV (Comma-Separated Values)
*   **Encoding:** UTF-8
*   **Delimiter:** Comma
*   **Header Row:** Fixed column names provided by Wix; do **not** rename or remove columns when re-importing.
*   **Rows:**
    *   Each **product** and **variant** is a separate row.
    *   Variants share the same `handleId` as their parent product.
*   **Limits:** Up to **5,000 rows per export**.
*   **Digital products:** Cannot be exported/imported currently. [\[support.wix.com\]](https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-stores-exporting-your-product-list), [\[support.wix.com\]](https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-stores-importing-products-to-wix-stores)

***

## ✅ **Mandatory Columns**

*(These must exist in every row)*

*   **`handleId`** – Unique identifier for the product (can match SKU or internal ID).
*   **`name`** – Product name.
*   **`fieldType`** – Either `Product` or `Variant`.
*   **`price`** – Base price for the product or variant. [\[support.wix.com\]](https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-stores-importing-products-to-wix-stores), [\[nxdigital.co.uk\]](https://www.nxdigital.co.uk/post/what-is-handleid-and-fieldtype-in-wix-csv-uploads-a-guide-for-beginners)

***

## ✅ **Common Columns in Wix CSV**

*(Exact names from Wix template)*

*   `handleId` – Unique product/variant identifier.
*   `name` – Product name.
*   `fieldType` – `Product` or `Variant`.
*   `description` – Rich text description.
*   `sku` – SKU for product or variant.
*   `price` – Price in store currency.
*   `discount` – Discount amount or percentage.
*   `visible` – TRUE/FALSE (whether product is visible in store).
*   `inventory` – Quantity in stock (if inventory tracking enabled).
*   `weight` – Product weight for shipping.
*   `ribbon` – Promotional ribbon text.
*   `optionChoices` – For variants (e.g., `Size:24x36;Media:Metal`).
*   `productImageUrl` – Primary image URL.
*   `additionalImageUrls` – Comma-separated list of extra image URLs.
*   `seoTitle` – SEO meta title.
*   `seoDescription` – SEO meta description.
*   `collections` – Comma-separated collection names.
*   `tags` – Comma-separated tags.
*   `shipping` – Shipping rule reference.
*   `tax` – Tax group reference.
*   `customTextFields` – For personalization options. [\[support.wix.com\]](https://support.wix.com/en/article/wix-stores-importing-products-to-wix-stores), [\[dev.wix.com\]](https://dev.wix.com/docs/velo/apis/wix-stores-v2/products-collection-fields)

***

### 📌 Example Header Row

    handleId,name,fieldType,description,sku,price,discount,visible,inventory,weight,ribbon,optionChoices,productImageUrl,additionalImageUrls,seoTitle,seoDescription,collections,tags,shipping,tax,customTextFields

***

## ✅ **Behavior**

*   **Variants:** Must have `fieldType = Variant` and share the same `handleId` as the parent product.
*   **Unused columns:** Leave blank; do not delete.
*   **Encoding:** Save as UTF-8 CSV.
*   **Images:** Use full URLs; Wix will fetch them during import.
